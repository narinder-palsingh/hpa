FACILITIES:

  1. Officers' Mess: The Mess has 4 suites and 20 well equipped rooms with AC, Cable TV, Telephone and en suite baths. The lounge/dining hall can easily accommodate 100-125 guests.
    • Other Facilities in the Officers' Mess include Billiards/Snooker, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, Badminton.
  2. Catering: The Officers' Mess Kitchen is geared to cater to various tastes and all the meals are served in the dining hall. Tea is served in rooms.
  3. Health: The campus has a good health center with adequate number of doctors and other supporting staff.
  4. Library: The Academy boasts of a well stocked library with more than 24,000 books. All the important journals and magazines are regularly subscribed.
  5. Computer Lab : The Lab has a capacity to train 300 persons at a time on various computer applications.
  6. Simulator: Simulated firing from various weapons is regularly done by the trainees.
  7. Model Police Station: For a hands on experience and feel of Police Station.
  8. Harshwardhan Auditorium
  9. Deekshant Parade Ground.
  10. Rangshala
  11. Sardar Patel Conference Hall.
  12. Sports: Sports being an integral part of police training, the Academy has excellent facilities for Golf, Cricket, Volleyball. Basketball, Yoga, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, Badminton, Swimming, Equestrian and Snooker/ Billiards, Scatting Ground etc.
  13. Gymnasium: The spacious Gymnasium has all modern workstations alongwith treadmills in air conditioned space.
  14. Swimming Pool: An Olympic size swimming pool and a beautiful stadium are located in the campus.
  15. Golf Course: A lush green and well manicured 9 hole Golf course alongwith its Club House is functional.
  16. Cultural Activities: The open air theatre "Rangshala" is the nerve centre of all recreational and cultural activities of the Academy. There is a semi-indoor cinema hall with state of the art seating and sound systems.
  17. Cyber Crime.
  18. Museum.
  19. Skating.
  20. Language Lab.
  21. Motor vehicle Training School (with simulator)
  22. Banks, ATMs and Post offices are also available in the Campus.
