Welcome to HPA Board

The objective of the Academy is to impart professional training to police personnel; so as to develop qualities of command and leadership among them and to foster ideals of professional commitment in public service. The Training shall also improve their physical fitness, impart professional knowledge, the proficiency and keenness of the trainees and inculcate in them those habits of health, activity, discipline, self reliance. Cleanliness, dignity of labour, keen observation, courtesy and straightforwardness in the execution of their work, which are essential to a police officers.

Scope of Acadamy

  1. Basic Training to directly recruited police officers of all ranks.
  2. In-service training to police officers in order to enhance their professional competence and skill.
  3. Specialized training on important aspects of police work.
  4. Research and development of strategies and equipments for dealing with police problems and improving the efficiency of police force.
